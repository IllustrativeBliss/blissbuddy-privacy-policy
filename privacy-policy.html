1. Introduction

Welcome to BlissBuddy! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our WhatsApp chatbot. By using BlissBuddy, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Data Collection

We collect the following information to provide and improve our services:

  • Phone Number: Your WhatsApp phone number is collected to facilitate communication.
  • Messages: Any messages you send to the chatbot, including text, images, and preferences.
  • Order Details: Information related to your orders, such as product preferences, customization requests, and payment details.
  • Photos: High-quality photos uploaded for custom illustrations.

3. Data Usage

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Order Processing: To process and fulfill your orders, including custom illustrations.
  • Customer Support: To provide real-time support and address your queries.
  • Payment Processing: To generate payment links and confirm payments via Razorpay UPI.
  • Order Tracking: To provide real-time updates on your order status.
  • Improvement of Services: To analyze user interactions and improve the chatbot's functionality.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in the following ways:

  • Google Sheets: Order details, including your name, phone number, product preferences, and payment status, are logged in Google Sheets for order tracking and analytics.
  • WhatsApp Cloud API: Messages and interactions are processed and stored temporarily by the WhatsApp Cloud API.

We take appropriate measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • Payment Processing: Payment details are shared with Razorpay to facilitate UPI payments.
  • Google Sheets: Order details are stored in Google Sheets for internal use only.
